About us & FAQ

What is ChurchGo?

The global embrace of internet technologies has reconfigured the fabric of society for congregations in a contemporary context.

We have spent 2 years researching, and have just began developing an application that combines both beautiful content to consume, and resources that simplify engagement with Churches.

Our dedication to empower the Church community in the modern era, sees no limit and we want you be a part of it.

Is it good for me?

We believe the best way for help the the community is to ensure connection with Churches and members is stronger than ever.

As a Church, you will be joining a large network of Churches online, have access to simple & powerful tools.
Event better, the sky is the limit in gaining a membership following on a global scale.

As a Member, stay connected with not just one but unlimited Churches, and never miss a moment that enriches your life on the go.

How can I help?

Sharing is caring! Tell your friends, family and Church leaders.
Connect with us and keep those feedbacks coming.
